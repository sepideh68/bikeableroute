The first page you will see when open the app.

Select reports through overlay button.The reports are showing base on 3 category.

The menu has option for viewing your track or sending us feedback,etc.

See your history tracking with details. When you click on "See elevation on map" your route with the elevation will show up.

When tap and hold on the map the reporting page will show up.Also you are able to select the "Report Hazards"" button which will get your current location to send the report

Select the photo listed when tap or click on "Report Hazards", then select the severity of the risk and send the report. your report will be shown on the map.
